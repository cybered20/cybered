About us

We are currently a group of three amateurs interested in the vast field of cybersecurity. This is a page where we collect what we've learned so far. What challenges we solved, what boxes we cracked and how we did it. In this repo we provide guides to each other and ourselves, to understand (and document) how hacking is done. The list of projects is expanded as we complete tasks and challenges, or come up with guides to teach ourselves and each other different techniques and tools.
